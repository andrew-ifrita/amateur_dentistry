Meet Andrew

Your Friendly Neighborhood Dental Enthusiast

Hi, I'm Andrew! Ever since I was a kid watching YouTube tutorials, I knew I wanted to work with teeth. While dental school seemed like a lot of paperwork, I figured the best way to learn is by doing!

My journey started in my garage with a dream and a pair of pliers. Today, I've upgraded to a full toolkit and this snazzy apron (the red adds character).

When I'm not perfecting my craft, I enjoy collecting vintage dental equipment, watching surgery videos for tips, and assuring concerned neighbors that everything is fine.

Our motto: "How hard can it be?"
